Mt. Pleasant 2025 Housing Report

Goals

The 2025 Housing Report has been developed and updated to help decision-makers, developers, real estate agents, and residents better understand the current housing conditions, challenges, and opportunities. Aligned with the goals outlined in the City Master Plan, the report also serves as a guide for future developments and policies.

What This Report Covers

  • Housing Availability: Rental vs. ownership patterns, student housing influence.
  • Housing Affordability: Cost burdens, AMI comparisons, and rising rent trends.
  • Demographic Shifts: CMU students, workforce families, senior populations.
  • Recommendations: Focusing on housing production and quality through available resoureces.
